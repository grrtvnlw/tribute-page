Steve Jobs

"I want to put a ding in the universe."

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco, CA on February 24.
  • 1961 - The Jobs family moves to Mountain View, CA.
  • 1968 - Steve Jobs gets an internship at Hewlett-Packard.
  • 1970 - Jobs meets future Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak through a friend.
  • 1972 - Graduates from Homestead High School in Cupertino, CA and enrolls at Reed College in Portland, OR.
  • 1974 - Jobs begins a brief stint as an engineer at Atari, working on a single-player version of Pong.
  • 1976 - Along with Wozniak and Ronald Wayne co-founds Apple Computer and sells the Apple I for $666.66.
  • 1977 - Apple incorporates and releases the Apple II.
  • 1980 - Apple goes public; Jobs' net worth above $200 million.
  • 1980 - Apple introduces the MacIntosh, the first commercially successful personal computer with a graphical user interface.
  • 1985 - Jobs resigns as Apple chairman and starts NeXT Computer.
  • 1986 - Buys the Graphics Group division of Lucasfilm for $10 million which later becomes Pixar Animation Studios.
  • 1988 - NeXT releases its first computer.
  • 1995 - Becomes Pixar's president and CEO.
  • 1977 - Apple incorporates and releases the Apple II.

If you haven't found it yet, keep looking. Don't settle. As with all matters of the heart, you'll know when you find it."
- Steve Jobs, RIP
